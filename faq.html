  • Fund Transfer

  • What is UPI transfer?

    Unified Payments Interface (UPI) is an instant payment system developed by the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI), which allows you to instantly transfer money between any two parties' bank accounts.

  • What are the benefits of doing a UPI transaction?

    Enables instant fund transfer from 110+ banks. Instant Transfer for clients with e-collect banks. Quicker process to add funds.

  • How much time does it take for funds to be added to my account via UPI ?

    The funds are added to the trading account instantly, on successfully adding them through UPI.

  • What is the limit of fund transfer through UPI ?

    The limit on fund transfer through UPI is 1 lac per transaction, and total of 1 lac per day.

  • Mutual Funds

  • What is e-Mandate?

    As the name suggests, e-Mandate implies electronic mandate and so it is completely paperless. When you generate an e-Mandate. the exchange facilitates online approval of your mandate from your bank account via NPCI. In this process, you don't need to submit any physical duly signed mandate form instead you are reqired to e-Sign the mandate generated using Aadhaar OTP. To do this process, you are supposed to click on the link in the email triggered from the exchange.

  • What are the benefits of e-Mandate?

    Just 3-day approvals instead of 40+ days for the x-mandate. Experience paperless process. No more signing and sending a hard copy of the x-mandate. A 3-step process. Enter Amount. Verify email. Provide an eSignature. Highly secured process with Aadhaar verification.

  • I have purchased an SIP, by when will the units be credited to my account?

    Units will be credited into your demat a/c usually on T+2 working days. Payout of MF units can vary as per the schemes settlement cycle. MF Units can be checked in the holding section.

  • Investing and Trading

  • What is a Limit Order?

    A Limit Order is an order to buy or sell a stock at a specific price. A Buy Limit Order can only be executed at the limit price or lower, while a Sell Limit Order can only be executed at the limit price or higher. A limit order is not guaranteed to execute.

  • What is IOC Validity Type?

    An Immediate or Cancel (IOC) order allows a trading member to buy or sell a security as soon as the order is released in the market, failing which the order will be removed from the market. A partial match is possible for the order, and the unmatched portion of the order is cancelled immediately.

  • What is Day Order Type?

    A Day Order is an order to buy or sell a security that automatically expires, if not executed on the day the order was placed. A day order is an order that is good for that day only.

  • What is Day Order Type?
